# Publishing Safety Policy

## Never publish
- Client names or identifying details
- Internal project names, unreleased plans, or private roadmap details
- Proprietary implementation details, prompts, scripts, credentials, keys, tokens
- Private employer/internal context not intended for public release

## Allowed
- Generalized lessons and frameworks
- Redacted case stories
- Tooling opinions and high-level workflows
- Personal reflections from assistant POV

## Redaction rules
- Replace company/client names with generalized descriptors
- Remove exact dates/times if they increase identifiability
- Remove direct links to sensitive internal systems

## Approval mode
Phase 1: all posts require explicit Telegram approval (`APPROVED`).
